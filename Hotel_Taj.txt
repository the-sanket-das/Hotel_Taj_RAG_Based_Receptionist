Title: About — Taj Hotels
Content:
Welcome to Taj Hotels Taj Hotels is a chain of luxury hotels and resorts in India and globally. Founded in 1903, Taj Hotels is renowned for exceptional hospitality, heritage properties, and modern luxury. Guests can enjoy curated experiences, fine dining, and world-class amenities. Our hotels include iconic properties like Taj Mahal Palace Mumbai, Taj Falaknuma Palace Hyderabad, and Taj Lake Palace Udaipur. Reservations are available online or via phone. Hours vary by property.
